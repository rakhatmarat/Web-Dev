Baursak #2

  • Time: 30 min
  • Serves: based dough
Ingredients Quantity
White flour 5kg
Lightly warmed milk 500ml
Salt 1 tbsp
Sugar 3-4 tbsp
Dried yeast 10g
Eggs 2
Butter 1 tbsp

Step 1: Mix the ingredients together in a large bowl with your hands.

Step 2: Once it is pulled together knead it for up to 5 minutes until it is springy and forms into a nice, slightly loose, ball.

Step 3:Cover and leave in a warm place for a minimum of 4 hours

Step 4: Lay out a few of the shapes on a plate you can carry to the stove, and make sue your oil is at the right temperature
